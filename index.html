Built for the long game

Everything you need to document change honestly.

The photo is only half the story. Witness keeps the numbers, the feelings, and the context right beside it.

Ghost overlay technology

Line up your shot with a semi-transparent overlay of your last photo — opacity from 0–100%, with an optional grid for composition. No more guessing, no more crooked comparisons. It's what makes every before/after land.

Custom metrics

Weight, cost, size, mood, sleep — 30+ presets or your own. Photos tell part of the story; metrics capture how you felt and what it took.

Time-lapse playback

Your sequence becomes a video automatically. Adjust speed from 0.5× to 4×, then export and share the whole journey.

Notes on every photo

Leave a note beside any frame — what you changed, what it cost, what threw the numbers off. Context you'll be grateful for later.

Smart reminders

Daily, weekly, or custom intervals with morning, afternoon, or evening preferences. Consistency, without the nagging.
